Keropok Lekor

Description: A chewy traditional fish snack from Terengganu.

Ingredients: Fish, sago flour, salt, water.

Restaurant Recommendations: Kedai Keropok Lekor Tepi Laut, Pasar Payang.

Sata

Description: Grilled fish wrapped in banana leaves.

Ingredients: Fish, coconut, chili, banana leaves.

Restaurant Recommendations: Gerai Sata Kg. Cina.

Nasi Dagang

Description: Nasi Dagang is a signature dish of Terengganu made from fragrant steamed rice cooked with coconut milk and fenugreek, traditionally served with rich tuna or mackerel curry.

Ingredients: Rice, coconut milk, fish, curry spices.

Restaurant Recommendations: Nasi Dagang Pak Malau, Pasar Besar Kuala Terengganu.

Watch on YouTube
Pulut Panggang

Description: Pulut Panggang is a traditional snack made from glutinous rice filled with spicy coconut and fish mixture, wrapped in banana leaves and grilled over charcoal.

Ingredients: Glutinous rice, grated coconut, chili, fish, banana leaves.

Restaurant Recommendations: Kedai Pulut Panggang Kg. Cina.

Watch on YouTube
Laksam

Description: Laksam consists of soft rice noodle rolls served with thick, creamy fish gravy made from coconut milk, commonly enjoyed as a breakfast dish in Terengganu.

Ingredients: Rice flour, coconut milk, fish, spices.

Restaurant Recommendations: Gerai Laksam Wak Rahmat, Pasar Payang.

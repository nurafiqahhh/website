Mak Yong

A traditional dance-drama combining acting, dance, music, and dialogue. Recognised by UNESCO as an Intangible Cultural Heritage.

Wayang Kulit

Wayang Kulit Kelantan is a traditional shadow puppet performance that tells legendary stories through intricately crafted leather puppets. The performance is accompanied by traditional music and serves as both entertainment and a medium for moral storytelling.

Dikir Barat

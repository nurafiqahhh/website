Pantai Cahaya Bulan

Pantai Cahaya Bulan (PCB) is one of the most famous beaches in Kelantan. It offers a relaxing coastal atmosphere with beautiful sea views, cooling breezes, and food stalls nearby. The beach is popular among families, tourists, and locals for leisure activities and sunset views.

Siti Khadijah Market

Siti Khadijah Market is a vibrant traditional market in Kota Bharu, well-known for its women vendors. It offers local food, fresh produce, handicrafts, and daily necessities, reflecting the daily life and culture of Kelantan.

Kelantan State Museum

The Kelantan State Museum showcases the history and heritage of Kelantan through traditional costumes, ancient tools, and cultural artifacts, giving visitors a glimpse of the state's rich past.

Pantai Irama

Pantai Irama is located in Bachok and is known for its calm shoreline and clean beach. Ideal for families, the surrounding area has small food stalls and basic facilities for a relaxed visit.

Wat Phothivihan

Wat Phothivihan is a Buddhist temple famous for its large reclining Buddha statue. It reflects the religious diversity of Kelantan and attracts visitors interested in cultural and spiritual heritage.

Istana Jahar

Istana Jahar is a historic royal palace in Kota Bharu, now a museum showcasing royal traditions, cultural artifacts, and the heritage of the Kelantan Sultanate. Famous for intricate wooden carvings and Malay architecture.
